Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our library management SAAS platform

Our SAAS platform offers significant advantages over traditional library systems. First, it's cloud-based, eliminating the need for expensive on-site servers and IT infrastructure. Second, we provide automatic updates and maintenance, ensuring you always have the latest features and security patches. Third, our platform is accessible from anywhere with an internet connection, allowing for remote management. Finally, our subscription model means predictable monthly costs instead of large upfront investments, with the flexibility to scale as your library grows.

Our implementation process is designed to be efficient and minimally disruptive to your operations. For small to medium libraries, the typical implementation takes 2-4 weeks from initial setup to going live. Larger libraries or those with complex requirements may take 4-8 weeks. The process includes data migration, system configuration, staff training, and a testing phase. We assign a dedicated implementation specialist to guide you through each step, ensuring a smooth transition from your current system to our platform.

We offer comprehensive support across all our plans. Our Standard plan includes email support with 24-hour response times and access to our knowledge base. The Professional plan adds priority email support with 8-hour response times, live chat during business hours, and monthly check-in calls. Our Enterprise plan features 24/7 priority support, a dedicated account manager, and quarterly business reviews. All customers receive access to regular webinars, training materials, and system updates. We pride ourselves on responsive, knowledgeable support that ensures your library operations run smoothly.

Data security is our top priority. Our platform employs industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with international data protection standards like GDPR. We maintain multiple redundant backups of your data with automatic failover systems. All data is stored in secure, certified data centers with 99.9% uptime guarantees. We implement strict access controls, and our staff undergoes regular security training. We also provide detailed documentation of our security practices and can sign custom data processing agreements for enterprise clients.

Professional and Enterprise plans offer more advanced customization including custom fields, workflow adjustments, and integration with third-party systems. For Enterprise clients, we can develop custom modules or features specific to your library's unique requirements. Our implementation team works closely with you to understand your needs and configure the system accordingly, ensuring the platform works the way your library does, not the other way around.
