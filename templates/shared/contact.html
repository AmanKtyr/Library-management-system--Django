Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member of a library, you need to register an account on our platform, then visit the library's page and click on "Become a Member". You'll need to select a membership plan and complete the registration process.

The number of books you can borrow depends on your membership plan. Basic plans typically allow 3-5 books, while premium plans may allow up to 10 books at once.

If you return a book after the due date, you'll be charged a late fee. The fee is typically calculated per day and varies by library. You'll need to pay the fine before you can borrow more books.

If you're a library administrator interested in joining our network, please contact our team through the form on this page or email us directly. We'll guide you through the onboarding process.
